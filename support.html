Support & contact

Hit a bug, have a feature request, or need help with your account? We read every message and reply within a couple of business days.

Email us

The fastest way to reach us is email. Include a screenshot if it's a visual bug, and your username if it's account-related — we'll find your data faster.

✉ vigorfitnessmealtracker@gmail.com

In-app feedback

You can also send feedback straight from the app: Profile → Send Feedback. It lands in our queue alongside email.

Common questions

  • Delete my account? Profile → Delete My Account. Wipes everything immediately.
  • Export my data? Profile → Export My Data. JSON via the system share sheet.
  • Forgot which email I signed up with? Email us — we can look it up.
  • Wrong calorie estimate? Re-describe the meal with portion size ("100g", "1 cup"). If still off, tap the meal and edit the numbers manually.
  • App stuck after signing in? Force-quit the app and reopen. If it persists, email us with your device model + iOS/Android version.

Privacy & data

Read how we handle your data in our Privacy Policy and the Terms of Service. Your weight, height, and personal info are private to your account — friends only see your username, avatar, streak, and the titles of meals you log.
